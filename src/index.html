Plainledger

Welcome to plainledger

plainledger is a tiny, single-user ledger for viewing and annotating bank transactions. Import a .qif export from your bank, then tag transactions with categories and comments.

Your data lives only in this browser. There is no account, no sync, no cloud storage. If you clear your browser data, switch browsers, or use another device, your transactions and annotations will be gone.

Back up manually. Use Export JSON regularly to save a copy of your ledger, and Import JSON to restore it. This is the only way to avoid losing your work.

Privacy

Your financial data never leaves your browser. This site is served as static files from a Cloudflare Worker — there is no backend, no database, no analytics, and no third-party scripts. The app does not make a single network call with your transactions.

Issues & feedback

Source code and issue tracker: github.com/gjolly/plainledger. Feel free to open an issue if something is broken or confusing — though please keep in mind this is a personal project, and it is up to the maintainer to decide what gets addressed.
